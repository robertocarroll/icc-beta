Registry

The Registry is a neutral organ of the Court that provides services to all other Court organs so the Court can function and conduct fair and effective public proceedings. The Registry is responsible for general court management, security, public information and Outreach, court records, translation and interpretation, field office support, counsel support (including OPCD and OPCV), legal aid, support for victims to participate in proceedings and apply for reparations, as well as receive support and protection, and much more.
