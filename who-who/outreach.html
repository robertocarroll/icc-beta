Interacting with affected communities

While the Court engages with international audiences, it prioritizes the flow of information to victims and members of communities affected by crimes under the Court’s jurisdiction (“affected communities”). As these communities have their own information needs and are often far away from the seat of the Court, a number of Court staff members work within some of these communities to bridge the gap and make the Courtroom more accessible.
