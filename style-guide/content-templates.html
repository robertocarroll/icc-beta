Content types

The recommended template for each piece of content is specified in a column in the sitemap.

1. Landing template

Landing content template is for sections with lots of content and many possible journeys.

The following sections will use the landing template (customised to suit the needs of your organ or department). These examples use existing or example content to show possible structure and formatting, but take this opportunity to re-write, improve, edit and pare down content.

  • Presidency
  • Judges and Chambers
  • Office of the Prosecutor
  • Registry
  • Defence
  • Victims
  • Interacting with affected communities

2. Standard template

Standard content template (will work for most things).

Many sections will use the article template including all "How we work" sections and these ones:

  • Detention Centre
  • Witnesses
  • Our Building

These examples use existing or example content to show possible structure and formatting, but take this opportunity to re-write, improve, edit and pare down content.

3. Biography template

Biography content template will be used to let users find out more about the people at the highest levels of the Court. Here's an example of a biography.

4. Contact template

Contact content template will be used to direct questions and enquiries from users to the correct part of the Court. Never enter the same contact more than once. If in doubt, please ask. Here's an example of a contact.

5. How we work template

How we work content template is the link at the end of the overview on the landing template. Once you've provided a broad overview, you can go into more detail about your responsibilities. Here's an example of a how we work page.
