Content types

1. Landing template

Landing content template is for sections with lots of content and many possible journeys. The recommended template for each piece of content is specified in a column in the sitemap.

The following sections will use the landing template (customised to suit the needs of your organ or department). These examples use existing or sample content to show possible structure and formatting, but take this opportunity to re-write, improve, edit and pare down content.

  • Presidency
  • Judges and Chambers
  • Office of the Prosecutor
  • Registry
  • Defence
  • Victims
  • Interacting with affected communities

2. Standard template

Standard content template (will work for most things). The recommended template for each piece of content is specified in a column in the sitemap.

Many sections will use the article template including all "How we work" sections and these ones:

  • Detention Centre
  • Witnesses
  • Our Building

These examples use existing or sample content to show possible structure and formatting, but take this opportunity to re-write, improve, edit and pare down content.
